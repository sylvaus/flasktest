Quaternions

Here, I won't give a long explanation about quaternions and more especially unit quaternions but a list of useful formulae.

Quaternion

Definition

$$ \begin{align*} \mathbf{q} &= a + b\mathbf{i} + c\mathbf{j} + d\mathbf{k} &=& A\left(\cos{\frac{\theta}{2}} + \mathbf{v}\sin{\frac{\theta}{2}}\right) \\ &= Ae^{\mathbf{v}\frac{\theta}{2}} &=& \left[w \; x \; y \; z \right]^T \\ &= \left[q_0 \; q_1 \; q_2 \; q_3 \right]^T &=& \left[q_0 \; \mathbf{q}_v \right]^T \end{align*} $$

Operations

$$ \begin{align*} &\lVert \mathbf{q} \rVert &=& \quad q_0^2 + q_1^2 + q_2^2 + q_3^2 \\ &ln(\mathbf{q}) &=& \quad ln(\lVert \mathbf{q} \rVert) + \frac{\mathbf{q}_v}{\lVert \mathbf{q}_v \rVert} \arccos(\frac{q_0}{\mathbf{q}}) \\ &exp(\mathbf{q}) &=& \quad e^{q_0} + \left(\cos(\lVert \mathbf{q}_v \rVert) + \frac{\mathbf{q}_v}{\lVert \mathbf{q}_v \rVert} \sin(\lVert \mathbf{q}_v \rVert) \right) \\ &\mathbf{q} \cdot \mathbf{p} &=& \quad \begin{bmatrix} q_0 p_0 - \mathbf{q}_v \cdot \mathbf{p}_v \\ q_0 \mathbf{p}_v + p_0 \mathbf{q}_v + \mathbf{q}_v \times \mathbf{p}_v \end{bmatrix} \\ &conjugate(\mathbf{q}) &=& \quad \left[q_0 \; \text{-}\mathbf{q}_v \right]^T \\ &inverse(\mathbf{q}) &=& \quad \frac{conjugate(\mathbf{q})}{\lVert \mathbf{q} \rVert} \end{align*} $$

Unit Quaternion

Definition

$$ \begin{align*} \mathbf{q} &= a + b\mathbf{i} + c\mathbf{j} + d\mathbf{k} &=& \cos{\frac{\theta}{2}} + \mathbf{v}\sin{\frac{\theta}{2}} \\ &= e^{\mathbf{v}\frac{\theta}{2}} &=& \left[w \; x \; y \; z \right]^T \\ &= \left[q_0 \; q_1 \; q_2 \; q_3 \right]^T &=& \left[q_0 \; \mathbf{q}_v \right]^T \\ \text{where}\\ \left\lVert\mathbf{q}\right\rVert_2 &= a^2 + b^2 + c^2 + d^2 \\ &= q_0^2 + q_1^2 + q_2^2 + q_3^2 \\ &= w^2 + x^2 + y^2 + z^2 \\ &= 1 \end{align*} $$

Operations

$$ \begin{align*} &\lVert \mathbf{q} \rVert &=& \quad 1 \\ &ln(\mathbf{q}) &=& \quad \frac{\mathbf{q}_v}{\lVert \mathbf{q}_v \rVert} \arccos(q_0)) = \arctan(\frac{q_0}{\mathbf{q}_v})\\ &exp(\mathbf{q}) &=& \quad e^{q_0} + \left(\cos(\lVert \mathbf{q}_v \rVert) + \frac{\mathbf{q}_v}{\lVert \mathbf{q}_v \rVert} \sin(\lVert \mathbf{q}_v \rVert) \right) \\ &\mathbf{q} \cdot \mathbf{p} &=& \quad \begin{bmatrix} q_0 p_0 - \mathbf{q}_v \cdot \mathbf{p}_v \\ q_0 \mathbf{p}_v + p_0 \mathbf{q}_v + \mathbf{q}_v \times \mathbf{p}_v \end{bmatrix} \\ &conjugate(\mathbf{q}) &=& \quad \left[q_0 \; \text{-}\mathbf{q}_v \right]^T \\ &inverse(\mathbf{q}) &=& \quad conjugate(\mathbf{q}) \end{align*} $$
